FAQ

Any Questions? Look Here

Specially developed App tailored to your eCTD business needs

Who uses this eCTD solution?

Regulatory affairs professionals, submission managers, publishers, and quality assurance teams in pharma, biotech, and CROs (Contract Research Organizations).

What file formats does the system support?

PDF, XML, and most document types accepted by CTD structures. The app automatically checks for technical conformance with eCTD validation rules.

Can I manage multiple regions (like US, EU, and Japan) from one platform?

Yes. The app supports multi-regional submission templates that align with ICH guidelines, allowing teams to reuse content across regions efficiently.

How is my data stored & secured?

Depending on your deployment preference, data can be stored on-premises or securely on cloud servers compliant with ISO 27001 and GDPR. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). Access is role-based, ensuring only authorized users can view or modify submissions.
